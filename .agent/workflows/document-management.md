---
description: Accessing and downloading financial documents like receipts and statements
---

This workflow is for accessing and managing all financial documents within the system, available to administrators and investment consultants.

### Steps:

1. **Navigate to My Documents**: Click on **My Documents** in the sidebar.
2. **View All Transactions**: You will see a unified list of all financial records, including rent receipts, water bills, and service charges.
3. **Search and Filter**:
   - Use the **search bar** to find a specific document by its title.
   - Use the **Filter** dropdown to narrow down documents by type (e.g., "Rent Receipt").
   - Use the **Sort** dropdown to order documents by date.
4. **Download a PDF**:
   - For any document in the list, click the **Download PDF** button.
   - A professionally formatted PDF of the selected receipt or statement will be generated and downloaded to your device.
5. **Export to CSV**:
   - Click the **Export CSV** button to download a spreadsheet of all documents currently visible in the filtered list.
